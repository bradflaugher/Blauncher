<p><b>Blauncher is a personal minimalist Android launcher fork.</b></p><p>It keeps the home screen focused, text-first, and free of advertising or promotional links.</p><p><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Minimal home screen without app icons</li><li>Rename and hide apps</li><li>Adjust text size, alignment, theme, date, and status bar visibility</li><li>Swipe gestures for apps, search, and notifications</li><li>Optional double tap to lock</li><li>Work profile, dual app, and private space support where available</li></ul><p>Blauncher does not provide an online wallpaper feed. You can choose any wallpaper through Android settings or your gallery app.</p><p><b>Privacy:</b> Blauncher does not collect or share personal data. The optional Accessibility Service is used only for the double tap to lock gesture.</p><p>This build is maintained as a personal fork and is provided without upstream promotional services.</p>
